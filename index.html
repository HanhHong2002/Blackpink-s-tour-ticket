ABOUT BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK in your area

BLACKPINK (stylized BLΛƆKPIИK) (블랙핑크) is a South Korean girl group under YG Entertainment. The members are Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They have a versatile sound, ranging from YG’s signature hardcore hip-hop to slower songs bordering on A-town trap. BLACKPINK officially debuted on August 8, 2016, releasing a two-track project titled Square One, featuring “BOOMBAYAH” and “Whistle.” The singles charted at #1 and #2 on Billboard’s World Digital Songs chart; and BLACKPINK are by far the fastest act to do so. On November 1, 2016, following the success of Square One, they released their second project, Square Two, which included “Playing with Fire” and “Stay.”
